About Salvador Villalon

My name is Salvador Villalon and I am a Junior at the University of California, Irvine studying Computer Science

My love for technology began when I designed and created my first website for the Administrative Intern Program. After finishing this project, I reliaze how important technology is. This project may have been small, but it was doing two important things that a Software Engineer always does. This website was introducing students to a program that can enhance their college and professional careers. At the same time, it was helping increase the brand and awareness of the Administrative Intern Program.

I realize that this is exactly what a Software Engineer does. They create the tools that organizations and people need to move forward. Thanks to this project, it guide me on my goal of becoming a Software Engineer.
